Using Spotify's API I will be able to analyze, automate, and predict my music listening experience.

Motivation.

Listening to music is a huge part of my daily life. Whether it's working out, partying, or for overall enjoyment, music is a necesity for myself. While Spotify is one of the largest music platforms for listening and exploring new music, I find many issues with the platforms ways of introducing new music or organizing my own music. Collecting data is important but visualizing it is better.

  • Creating a playlist from scratch can be tedious and time consuming
  • Songs that I listen search for will appear in Spotify's automated Daily Mix too frequently
  • Spotify's Daily Mix contain user liked songs more than other related or recommneded songs
  • Recommended music is either advertised or irrelevant to my music taste.
  • Create a Flask API
  • Create a React client
  • Display data,

During each listening session a connection is established to access the users Spotify Info of the current playing track. Under certain conditions, the songs details (date listned, song name, artist, features, genre) will be imported into my personal database for further analysis. Future analysis will forecast my listening behaviors and will create up to date playlists based on the information within the databse
